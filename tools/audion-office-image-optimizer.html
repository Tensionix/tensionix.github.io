Overview

What this tool is for and why it exists.

Audion Office Image Optimizer is designed for practical Office workflows where document and presentation images need to be processed in a cleaner, more controlled, and more repeatable way.

What it does

Main purpose and direction of the tool.

Office-focused image handling

Optimizes images for Office-oriented workflows such as DOCX and PPTX preparation.

Portable operation

Intended as a portable tool that can be used without turning the workflow into a dependency mess.

Repeatable results

Built around the idea of consistent output instead of manual one-off cleanup.
